PMD静态代码检查

maven插件：maven-pmd-plugin

pom.xml添加如下：

    <plugin>
            <groupId>org.apache.maven.plugins</groupId>
            <artifactId>maven-pmd-plugin</artifactId>
            <version>2.7.1</version>
            <executions>
                <execution>
                    <phase>package</phase>
                    <goals>
                        <goal>check</goal>
                    </goals>
                </execution>
            </executions>
            <configuration>
                <failurePriority>2</failurePriority>
                <targetJdk>1.6</targetJdk>
                <rulesets>
                    <ruleset>/pmd-rulesets.xml</ruleset>
                </rulesets>
            </configuration>
        </plugin>

其中pmd-rulesets.xml是规则文件

如何使用

mvn pmd:pmd
mvn pmd:check

pmd在eclipse中的使用
